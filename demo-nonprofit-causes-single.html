Objectives

  • Age-appropriate learning outcome for primary school children
  • Building capacity of youth to lead whole school transformation
  • Peer learning space for children in village
  • Improvement in ownership of community and Panchyat and Community

Ghar Ghar Pdhai

A youth lead delivers a learning kit to every child at her home. Through a whatsapp group, relevant lesson specific videos are sent to guide the children to complete 1-2 worksheets everyday.
The worksheets contain home based learning projects which are intended to make learning more contextual and experience based.
